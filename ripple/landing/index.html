LEMONVILLE

Run a business in a little lemon town — with your whole class.
🎟️ TAKE A SEAT
▶ JOIN THE GAME
Ask your teacher for the join link. It looks like http://THEIR-COMPUTER-IP:3001/
and you must be on the same Wi-Fi as their computer.
HOW IT WORKS
1️⃣ You get a business: a farm, a depot, a store or a café.
2️⃣ Each round: pick a price and an amount, press CONFIRM.
3️⃣ Watch the town react — your moves ripple through everyone else. 🦋
👩‍🏫 TEACHERS
Run npm run dev, then share the join URL it prints (or link players to this page with ?join=http://your-ip:3001/). Full guide in the README.
